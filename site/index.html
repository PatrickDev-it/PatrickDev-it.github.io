Operating principles

Professionalism is an evidence chain.

Architecture, product behavior, security, CI, deployment, and documentation must describe the same system.

01 / Claims

Evidence before adjectives

Metrics include their workload, environment, method, date, and reproduction command.

02 / Architecture

Contracts before providers

Cloud, data, storage, identity, queue, and AI vendors terminate at tested adapters.

03 / Operations

Failure is product behavior

Timeouts, retries, idempotency, migration, recovery, and rollback are designed explicitly.

DeployOps model

One artifact, progressively verified.

Portability comes from responsibility boundaries and contract tests, not duplicated application implementations for every provider.

  1. Source and tests
  2. Immutable artifact
  3. Provider adapters
  4. Ephemeral verification
  5. Observe and roll back
Deployment claims are tiered.

Reproducible, container-portable, platform-portable, provider-portable, and operationally verified are separate claims with separate evidence.
